The Experience

Survival

Scavenge for food, water, and medical supplies. Manage your health, hunger, and thirst as you navigate the harsh post-apocalyptic landscape.

About ReforgedZ

Our Story

ReforgedZ was born from a love for the original Arma 2 DayZ mod - the tension of not knowing if the next player you meet is a friend or a bullet. We set out to recreate that feeling on Arma Reforger's Enfusion engine, building every system from scratch to deliver an authentic DayZ-inspired survival experience.

What Makes Us Different

Everything is custom. Our zombie AI, loot economy, medical system, basebuilding, vehicles, questing, humanity/faction system, and trader networks are all built specifically for Reforger. We don't port - we rebuild. The result is something that feels familiar but plays like a completely new experience.

The Team

A small, dedicated group of modders and developers who spend way too much time in the Enfusion Workbench. We're passionate about the project and actively develop new features, squash bugs, and push updates regularly. Community feedback drives our roadmap.

The Vision

Build the definitive DayZ-style experience on Arma Reforger. Hardcore survival, meaningful player interaction, faction-driven content, and a world that feels alive and dangerous. Every update gets us closer.
